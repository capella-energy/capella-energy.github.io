Our Story

We are Capella, a startup business launched by Bemin Shaker and Alex Gaskins, two engineering students who are on a mission to create a solution that empowers people to live in more sustainable and energy efficient homes by making renewable energy more accessible to everyone. We want to revolutionize the way people view energy usage by using the power of technology to make this process both easy and inexpensive. Our hope is that households across America will be invested in a simple and effective lifestyle. With the help of our associates at LaunchPad, we hope to make this goal possible!
